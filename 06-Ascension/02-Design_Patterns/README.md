# The Wisdom of Patterns: Design Patterns

*"Design patterns are not code — they are concepts. They are the distilled wisdom of thousands of architects who came before you, crystallized into reusable solutions to common problems. A pattern is a blueprint for solving a recurring design challenge."*

---

## **What Are Design Patterns?**

Design patterns are **proven, named solutions** to problems that appear repeatedly in software design. They were cataloged by the "Gang of Four" (GoF) in 1994 and remain essential knowledge for every software architect.

## **The Three Categories**

| Category | Purpose | Patterns You'll Learn |
|----------|---------|----------------------|
| **Creational** | How objects are created | Singleton, Factory Method |
| **Structural** | How objects are composed | Decorator |
| **Behavioral** | How objects communicate | Observer |

## **Your Path**

1. **`a-Creational_Singleton/`** — Ensuring exactly one instance
2. **`b-Creational_Factory_Method/`** — Creating objects without specifying exact classes
3. **`c-Structural_Decorator/`** — Adding behavior dynamically
4. **`d-Behavioral_Observer/`** — Notifying dependents of changes

*"Each pattern is a tool in your architect's belt. Know when to use each, and your designs will be elegant and resilient."*
