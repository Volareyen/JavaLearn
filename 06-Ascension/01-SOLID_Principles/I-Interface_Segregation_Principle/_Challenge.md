# Trial: ISP — Smart Home Device Interfaces

Refactor a fat `SmartDevice` interface (turnOn, turnOff, setTemperature, playMusic, setAlarm, stream, dim) into focused interfaces. Implement: `SmartSpeaker` (audio only), `SmartThermostat` (temperature only), `SmartLight` (on/off and dimming), and `SmartHub` (all capabilities).
